Twitter 2nd Line Followers

This is a very simple web app that connects to your Twitter account using OAuth and displays a list of your followers' followers. It also shows you the number of your own followers that each 2nd line follower follows.

For that we first need to connect the app through Twitter. This will only grant the app read privileges and you can revoke access at any time from your account.
